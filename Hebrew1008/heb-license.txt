          The Hebrew Bible with Lemma and Morphology Data

The Leningrad Codex of the Hebrew Bible is in the public domain.
Although the digital base text originates through the Open Scriptures
Hebrew Bible from the Westminster Leningrad Codex, it has not been
verified and does not present the content of WLC precisely.  You may
use the current digital base text version of Leningrad Codex in this
corpus at your own risk.

The Hebrew morphological analysis and lemmas have been derived from
the Open Scriptures Hebrew Bible.  The morphological codes has been
replaced with Leipzig-style morphological categoes.  The translation
may contain errors and you may use it only at your own risk.  

Lemma and morphology data are licensed under a Creative Commons
Attribution 4.0 International license. For attribution purposes,
credit the Open Scriptures Hebrew Bible Project. The text of the WLC
remains in the Public Domain.

The transliteration has been developed in the spirit of the SBL
transliteration standard.  However, the transliteration is needs to be
still improved.  You may use the transliteration only at your own
risk.
